# Vocal Integration Reference

## Target Style: Cloonee & InntRaw - "Stephanie"

**Original:** Young M.A. - "OOOUUU" (hip-hop)
**Remix:** Cloonee & InntRaw - "Stephanie" (tech house, 130 BPM)
**Label:** HELLBENT (2024)

### Key Characteristics
- Takes hip-hop vocals from Young M.A.
- Layers over deep bass-heavy tech house production
- Vocals featured prominently during breakdowns/builds
- Maintains vocal energy while completely transforming instrumental

### Our Approach
1. Find MF DOOM or Megan Thee Stallion track matching our key (or pitch-shift)
2. Extract clean vocal stems
3. Place vocals during breakdown sections (not over drops)
4. Let the bass and drums dominate the drops
5. Use vocals to build tension and provide narrative
